LOGIN QUESTIONS

What do I do if I don't have an account?

Register

In the login form, you can click where it asks if you don't have an account, sending you to the registration form.

What do I need to do to register?

Enter your details

Open the registration form and fill in at least the fields for Username, name, email, and password.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is Gestionis safe?

Yes, it is

Any data you enter in our application will never leave it, making it safe to input your earnings and expenses without worry.

How do I level up?

Save

Each time you manage to save money using our app, you will gain experience to enjoy the advanced features of the app.

How do I unlock investments?

Level up

To unlock features like the ranking or the investment manager, you need to reach a certain user level by saving money daily.

How do I change my profile?

Your account

In the top right corner of the main form, there will be an option with your profile picture. Clicking it will open your profile form, where you can change anything you want.

How do I record an expense?

Record expense

In the main form, you can see two buttons to record an expense or an income; simply fill in the required information and you can add it.
